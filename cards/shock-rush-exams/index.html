Causes of Elevated Lactate

High lactate levels associated with worse prognosis in medical and trauma patients

  • Level >2 mmol/L is abnormal
  • Level >4 mmol/L suggests tissue hypoperfusion

Causes for elevation in lactate that is NOT related to shock

Pathology Causes

Inadequate
O2 delivery
  • Severe dehydration
  • Blood loss
  • Severe anemia
  • Hypoxemia
  • CO prolonged exposure
  • Trauma

Disproportionate
O2 demand
  • Hyperthermia
  • Shivering
  • Seizure
  • Strenuous exercise

Inadequate
O2 use
 SIRS
  • DM
  • TPN
  • HIV infection
  • Medications:
    • Metformin
    • Salicyclate
    • Antiretrovirals
    • INH
    • Propofol
    • Cyanide
